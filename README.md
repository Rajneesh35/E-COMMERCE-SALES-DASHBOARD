ğŸ“Œ KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)

Based on the dashboard, the following KPIs are highlighted:

1. Total Revenue

The total monetary value generated from all customer sales

2. Total Orders

Represents the total number of customer orders recorded.

3. Profit Margin %

Calculated as:
Profit Margin = Total Profit / Total Revenue

4. Average Order Value (AOV)

Formula:
AOV = Total Revenue / Total Orders

5. Total Profit

Shows the overall profit earned from all transactions.

These KPIs give a high-level understanding of the companyâ€™s financial performance.
